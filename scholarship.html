Scholarship Details

Scholarship test details

Take the test at a date and time of your choice

Timings : 9AM to 10PM Daily

Duration : 3 hrs

Eligibility Criteria : Class 10th, 11th & Class 12th(repeater) passed students

Mode : Offline (You can give exam in any branch of EduCave)

A student is allowed a maximum of two attempts in a year at the EduTest

Exam Pattern

Maximum marks : 120

Marking scheme : +1 (for every correct attempted question) | -0.25 (for every wrong attempted question) | 0 (for every unattempted question)

Syllabus : Class 10th Maths and Science

Section 1 : Maths (40 Marks)

Section 2 : Science (40 Marks)

Section 3 : Logical Thinking (40 Marks)

Offering Criteria

  • Students having >110 marks will be offered 100% scholarship
  • Students having >100 marks will be offered 90% scholarship
  • Students having >90 marks will be offered 75% scholarship
  • Students having >75 marks will be offered 50% scholarship
  • Students having >50 marks will be offered 25% scholarship
  • No scholarships will be offered for students having <50 marks

Test Highlights

  • Completely online ensuring the safety and convenience of all candidates
  • Flexible timings - students can choose and select any time slot between 9AM to 10PM
  • Conducted everyday so that students can schedule the test as per their convenience
  • Scholarships will be availed on 1,2,3 & 4 Year Integrated Classroom Courses for NEET, JEE, CBSE & Foundations
  • Result will be available on EduTutor app within the two days of test given
